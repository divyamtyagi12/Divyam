It's uncertain if Rohit Sharma will play in the 2027 Cricket World Cup. While reports suggest he and Virat Kohli might not be in the team's plans for 2027, with some indicating they could retire from ODIs in late 2025, Sharma himself has stated he is keeping his options open and will continue to play as long as he enjoys it and performs well. His future participation depends on his form and fitness, and potentially his willingness to play domestic tournaments like the Vijay Hazare Trophy to stay in contention.
